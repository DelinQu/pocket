Photos are necessary

Maybe some people think that it is hypocritical to post photos of people after exercise, running, swimming and so on.But I'm telling you today, not at all!Most people post photos to motivate themselves and make them more determined to continue their exercise. What they gain is not only sweat, but also happiness.Some studies have shown that people who regularly post moments and QQ updates to celebrate have better executive function and better training, which is why some people like to post photos.This is not a publicity stunt but a way to keep going.
